I am Dr. Sidhanti Somprakash Nyahatkar, a qualified dental surgeon with a Bachelor in Dental Surgery (BDS) degree from Vidharbha Youth Welfare Society Dental College and Hospital. As an Associate Researcher at Neuron Institute of Applied Research in Amravati, I have produced over 30 high-impact research papers, reviews, meta-analyses, case reports, and patents.

My research expertise is primarily focused on exosomes and oral oncology, for which I've been recognized as a guest speaker at international conferences in Paris, Barcelona, Italy, and London. I received the Youngest Scientist Award at the 3rd International Conference on Dentistry and Oral Health by Bioleague and Apaodent.

I have collaborated with prestigious institutions including Harvard University, AIIMS Delhi, Johns Hopkins, and Tata Memorial Hospital on cutting-edge research projects in oncology and exosome research. These collaborations have contributed to breakthrough findings in cancer diagnostics and treatment approaches.
